We are students of the Cisco Networking Academy at the Chesterfield Career and Technical Center! Every year, the second-year class is challenged with organizing and then executing a gaming tournament. This allows us to test our IT and project management skills, as well as raise some funds for the program!


Students in the Cisco Networking Academy have many opportunities, such as:

•Developing advanced skills in computer hardware, networking, and cybersecurity
•Competing in numerous cybersecurity competitions
•Earning industry certifications, such as the A+ and CCNA
•Attending and hosting events, such as the gaming tournament


If you're a high school student interested in IT, then the Cisco Networking Academy is a great way to expand your knowledge. For more information, visit the official CCPS Tech Center website.
