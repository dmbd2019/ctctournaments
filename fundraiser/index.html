The gaming tournament helps raise funds for the Cisco Networking Academy at CTC. In addition to the tournament itself, we also host a giveaway during the event. Every year, students of the Cisco Networking Academy design and build a gaming PC to be given away. Tickets can be purchased from students before and during the event, and they give you a chance at winning the PC. The PC's specs are listed below.

Estimated value of around $3,000

Single tickets can be bought for $7, or two tickets for $10. These tickets will then be drawn at the event. You are not required to attend the event to win the PC. 10% of all funds gathered from the fundraiser will go to Project ASK, a non-profit organization that supports children with cancer and their families. All other proceeds benefit the Cisco Networking Academy, and allow us to continue hosting events such as the gaming tournament.
