Welcome to the official CCTC Gaming Tournament XIII website! Here you can find more information about the event such as prices, registration, and sponsors. To register your team, view fundraiser information, read about our program, or look at the staff list, use the navigation bar above.


The tournament will be comprised of multiple 5 vs 5 games taking place on Summoner's Rift. A full team of five players is not required, but it is heavily recommended. Teams will be given prizes based on their ranking, and members of the winning team will each get a prize.


Those who aren't competing in the main tournament can stay occupied with the side games. This year's tournament will have a Super Smash Bros. Ulimate tournament on the side. The winner will receive a Nintendo Switch Lite. There will be another station open for casual play as well, which doesn't require a fee. Other side games include Mario Kart 8 Deluxe and VR.
