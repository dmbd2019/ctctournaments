When competitors arrive at the tournament, they will sign in and set up at their assigned table. Keep in mind that competitors must bring their own computers, peripherals, and cables. Computers must have an Ethernet port and the latest version of League of Legends.


Those interested in competing in the Super Smash Bros. Ultimate side tournament must pay a fee of $10 in cash (including entrance fee). Only walk-in registration is available. The first place competitor will win a Nintendo Switch Lite, with second and third place competitors receiving Nintendo eShop gift cards. Controllers will be limited, so bring your own controller if you are concerned.

The event is located at 10101 Courthouse Rd, Chesterfield, VA 23832.
